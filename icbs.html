ICBS (Internal Core Banking System)

iCBS (Internal Core Banking System) is a live production system utilized by six prominent local banks in Myanmar for their daily transactional operations. As a comprehensive banking solution, it encompasses all functionalities requisite for efficient banking operations, ensuring seamless transactions and robust customer service. Continuously maintained to accommodate evolving customer needs, iCBS stands as a cornerstone in the digital transformation of the banking industry in Myanmar, providing a robust and reliable platform for daily transactions and customer service. Its continuous maintenance and adaptability underscore its commitment to excellence and innovation in serving the evolving needs of the banking sector.

Key Features For about Development and Maintainence

Comprehensive Functionality

iCBS offers a wide array of functionalities essential for banking operations, including account management, fund transfers, loan processing, and customer relationship management.

Real-time Transaction Support

With real-time transaction capabilities, iCBS ensures swift and accurate processing of transactions, enhancing operational efficiency and customer satisfaction.

System Implementation and Customization

Oversaw the customization of iCBS to accommodate unique bank-specific requirements, such as localized reporting formats, transactional workflows, and regulatory compliance frameworks.

Customization and Maintenance

The system is continuously maintained to address customer requirements promptly. Customization options allow for tailored solutions, ensuring optimal performance and satisfaction.

Robust Reporting

iCBS generates comprehensive reports essential for banking operations, facilitating informed decision-making and regulatory compliance.

Reporting System using PL/SQL and Oracle Analytical Publisher

Development Features

This project is about to support Bank with the Reports using PL/SQL and Oracle Analytical Publisher. In this project, reports are made with Data Models using Microsoft Publisher Extension on Microsoft Word. This project mainly supports all reports that are very important to a Bank Live Production. Reports include Financial reports, Daily reports, Monthly report, Yearly Report and other’s Bank Statements reports like that.
